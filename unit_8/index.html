About Claire

Design enthusiast, art appreciator and innovation seeker. Claire is passionate about visally communicating ideas, through smart design, to convey beauty and meaning. Engaging with others through design is what she loves.

Claire is driven by a passion to excel, to continue to innovate and learn both at work and in other aspects of life. Problem solving is a key strength, seen through the way projects and teams are managed. Tasks are completed with effiency and high attention to detail.

With a practical and creative approach to every project, Claire has become a key contributor in each business. Mastering new programs and providing training to others is an ongoing strength.

Skills

Claire has many key skills across the fields of art and design. She is considered an Adobe Photoshop and InDesign expert, with a strong understanding of user centered design.

Currently working as the Graphic Design Coordinator for an Australian swimwear label, Claire oversees the design and production of print and web collateral. This includes printed range books, interactive digital catalogues, membership emails, sections of the website, social media banners and more.

Outside of work, Claire focuses on further developing her front end web development skills. She is an amateur photographer and illustrator, with an emphasis on the digital aspect of each.
